
# AAA Creature Design: The Resonance Weaver (Evolution of The Threadling)

## 1. Core Concept
The Resonance Weaver is a 20-meter-tall predatory entity that acts as the "immune system" of the REDROOM. It is blind, sound-sensitive, and moves via inverted suspension. It does not exist *in* the room so much as it *occupies* it.

## 2. Silhouette & Anatomy
- **The Hub:** A cluster of three translucent resonance bladders (pale, flayed muscle textures). They pulse with the player's heartbeat.
- **The Needles:** 14 asymmetrical, multi-jointed limbs. They appear like weaver's needles.
- **The Form:** In the cramped rooms, it appears "folded." Limbs are constantly shifting, twitching, and re-arranging.
- **No Face:** A flat, bone-like surface with porous holes for sound intake.

## 3. Sensory Organs: Acoustic Resonance
- **Resonance sacs:** These vibrate visibly. If the sac turns bright red, the creature has locked onto the sound source.
- **Vibration Sensitivity:** It perceives the world as a 3D map of vibrations.

## 4. Locomotion: Threaded Suspension
The Weaver threads its limbs into the organic architecture of the maze. It can exist on the ceiling, walls, or floor simultaneously. 

## 5. Movement Studies
- **Idle:** Limbs twitch slowly; resonance sacs pulsate rhythmically.
- **Detection:** The entire creature freezes. The resonance sacs swell and turn a deep crimson.
- **Hunt:** The creature "unfolds," its 20m span visible as it twitches across the ceiling at high speed toward the player.

## 6. Scale Comparison
- **Human:** 1.7m
- **Resonance Weaver:** 20m (Folded into 6m vertical space)

## 7. Variants
- **A: The Stalker:** Moves dynamically across all surfaces.
- **B: The Ceiling Dweller:** Hangs sensory tendrils downward.
- **C: The Hallway Watcher:** Spans the width of corridors.
- **D: The Final Form:** A room-filling mass of resonance bladders.
